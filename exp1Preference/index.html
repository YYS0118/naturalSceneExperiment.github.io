Welcome to Our Study on Visual Preference


Overview:


Welcome to our study on visual preference, which focuses on understanding chromatic scene statistics and aesthetics.
This research aims to explore how different visual elements influence aesthetic preferences.
Your participation will help contribute to the body of knowledge in visual perception and preferences.
